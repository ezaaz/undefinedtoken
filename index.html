About

I’m a designer who’s spent more than a decade bridging the gap between “it works” and “it actually looks good.”

Currently, I am leading Visual Experience at Swiggy in Bengaluru, where I focus on the interaction and storytelling of the consumer experience. My job is to make sure everything flows seamlessly from the things you see to the things you feel, turning every app visit into a delightful experience.

Before this, I led design at WeWork India and wrote code for The Glitch. When the logic brain takes a break, I’m usually drawing cats, credit-maxxing on Claude, or 3D-printing a small army of toy characters.
